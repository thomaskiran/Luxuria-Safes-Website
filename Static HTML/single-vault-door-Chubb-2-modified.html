Security Vault Doors

Chubb 2" Strongroom Door

The Chubbsafes 2” Strongroom Door is a reliable, entry-level high-security vault door designed for commercial and institutional applications. It combines robust construction with proven locking systems to safeguard cash, documents, and valuables.

Burglary
Fire

Key Features

  • Certified Protection:
    Designed for applications requiring certified burglary and fire protection.
  • Fire Protection
    Offers high-level thermal protection for up to 120 minutes.
  • Reliable Locking
    Dual locking system provides a redundant layer of security.

Construction

  • Anti-Tamper Protection
    Equipped with a glass relocker and drill-resistant protection plates.
  • Robust Boltwork
    Features a comprehensive multi-directional bolt system for maximum door-to-frame integrity.
  • Reinforced Barrier
    Utilizes specialized Chubbsafes F80 barrier material reinforced with steel fibers for physical attack resistance.

Applications

  • Commercial Vaults
    Ideal for retail, and commercial environments requiring secure storage solutions.
  • Cash Handling Facilities
    Perfect for cash centers and facilities that require secure access control and fire protection.
  • Document Protection
    Provides secure storage for sensitive documents and valuables in various settings.
