A2P-Certified Locks

All our safe locks are certified A2P in accordance with the European standard EN 1300, a standard dedicated to high-security locks for safes. EN 1300 classifies locks into 4 levels of security against break-ins: A, B, C, and D (D being the highest level of resistance to break-ins). The classification is based on the observed resistance following intrusion tests conducted by the laboratory of the National Center for Prevention and Protection (CNPP).

Key Locks (Grade A)

Description:

  • “Very high-security” electronic lock
  • User-friendly operation with a graphical screen
  • Allows the creation of up to 39 users by default, extendable to 99 users with the configuration software

Security Feature:

  • Option for delayed opening (from 0 to 99 minutes)
  • Temporary blockage in case of entering a wrong code
  • Safe locking in case of battery removal (unlocking with the code)
