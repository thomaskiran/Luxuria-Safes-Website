MiniTreasure

A certified luxury home safe created for collectors of jewellery, watches, and valuable personal items. Combining tested security with handcrafted interiors, it integrates seamlessly into wardrobes, dressing rooms, and luxury interiors while offering reliable protection against burglary and fire.

Specifications

  • Burglary Resistance: ECB-S Certified EN 14450 – S2
  • Fire Protection: ECB-S Certified EN 15659 – 30 minutes protection for documents
  • Locking System: ECB-S Certified EN 1300 Keylock / Electronic Lock
  • Interior: Custom Alcantara & Spanish wood
  • Volume: 35 to 90 L
  • Weight: 41 to 72 kg
